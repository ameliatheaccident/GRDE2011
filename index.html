About Us

What is the Lilac Valley Farm Stay?

Situated in the Blue Mountains, Katoomba, The Lilac Valley Farm Stay satisfies your rural getaway needs by providing a comfortable place to stay.

With beautiful views of the mountains in the distance their are various local activities to do that surround you in the breathtaking wilderness of the Blue Mountains.

Say hello to our resident farm animals. We have sheep, chickens, ducks, and a goat named Rosie who will come up to you and want to be your friend.

Local Attractions

Scenic World

The steepest railway in the world, Scenic World is one of the most beautiful places to visit in the Blue Mountains.

Three Sisters

The Three Sisters are the most known attraction in the Blue Mountains and are a must see for first timers.

Blue Mountains Botanic Gardens

This Botanic Gardens has a wide variety of plants from around the world that are used to the climate of the Blue Mountains.

The Wintergarden

A restaurant overlooking the Megalong Valley, The Wintergarden is the perfect place to have a delicious meal.

Lost Bear Gallery

For those who love contemporary art, the Lost Bear Gallery is the place for you.

Blue Mountains Cultural Centre

Includes an art gallery, Katoomba library and the Into the Blue exhibition.

Blue Mountains Theatre + Community Hub

Sister facility to the Cultural Centre, this is a place for people to come together and enjoy art forms and making bonds.

Katoomba

Katoomba is situated in the middle of the Blue Mountains and has many different places to visit all showcased on the Heritage and Art Walk map.
